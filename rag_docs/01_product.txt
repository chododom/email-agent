Philips L'Oréa 9000 Pro Espresso System

The Philips L'Oréa 9000 Pro is the culmination of decades of Dutch engineering and Italian coffee passion. This fully automatic espresso system is designed for the discerning home barista, offering unparalleled customization and one-touch convenience. From a robust espresso to a perfectly layered latte macchiato, the L'Oréa 9000 Pro delivers a cafe-quality experience directly to your countertop. Its sleek, brushed platinum finish and intuitive Chroma-Touch display make it a stunning centerpiece for any modern kitchen.

Key FeaturesAromaSelect Grinder: Features 100% durable ceramic burrs with 18 precise grind settings, ensuring maximum flavor extraction from any bean type. LattePerfection System: A seamless, integrated milk carafe that uses cyclonic frothing technology to create dense, velvety foam for cappuccinos and lattes, easily detachable for refrigerator storage. AquaClean 3.0 Filter: Includes an advanced water filtration system that guarantees up to 7,500 cups without the need for descaling (based on eight $8 \text{ oz}$ cups per day). Smart Recipe Memory: Stores up to 12 personalized user profiles, each capable of saving preferences for coffee length, strength, temperature, and foam level. Opti-Temp Boiler: Dual thermo-block heating system ensures that the coffee water is heated precisely to $95^{\circ} \text{C}$ and the steam for milk is instantly ready, minimizing wait time.
